# Product Context — Rua do Saber

## Product Vision

Rua do Saber is a continuous traffic education program for elementary school students.

The platform combines:

- Classroom teaching
- Digital content
- Interactive challenges
- Real-world missions

The goal is to form children who understand traffic, make safe decisions, and influence their communities.

---

## Pedagogical Journey

The program spans five years of learning.

### Year 1 — Discover the Street
Students learn to observe and understand their urban environment.

### Year 2 — Read the Traffic System
Students interpret signals, signs and markings.

### Year 3 — Build Safe Habits
Students transform knowledge into automatic safe behavior.

### Year 4 — Evaluate Risk
Students develop judgment and decision-making skills.

### Year 5 — Citizenship
Students understand traffic as part of society and become responsible participants.

---

## Gamification Philosophy

Gamification reinforces progress but avoids unhealthy competition.

Allowed:
- XP
- Levels
- Collectible cards
- Visual rewards
- Journey progression

Avoid:
- Competitive rankings
- Daily streak pressure
- Punishment mechanics

---

## Role of the Mascot

The robot mascot bridges teacher explanation, platform activity and student engagement.

Mascot responsibilities:

- Introduce lessons
- Explain activities
- Deliver rewards
- Present missions
